<objective>
Land the `[telephony]` config loader deferred from Phase 10 D-09, and widen `config.py`'s credential-field rejection so the §24 secret-looking fields (`pin`, `passphrase`, `words`) can never be silently accepted as TOML tunables.

Purpose: Every downstream telephony plan (controller, gate, entrypoint) reads a validated, frozen `TelephonyConfig`. The credential-regex widening is the structural guarantee (D-09) that §24 secrets stay in env/SSM, never TOML.
Output: `TelephonyConfig` dataclass + `load_telephony_config()` in `telephony/config.py`; widened `_CREDENTIAL_FIELD_RE`; a `[telephony]` table in `pipeline.toml` (enabled=false); credential-rejection + parse tests.
</objective>
