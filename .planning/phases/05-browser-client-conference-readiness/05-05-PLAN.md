<objective>
Layer the two session-awareness overlays onto the live stage: a small persistent **countdown timer**
(CLNT-05) that escalates near cutoff in sync with the agent's spoken -30s warning (D-10/QUOT-03), and the
**latency HUD** (CLNT-06/D-09) — off by default (pristine for audiences), toggled by tap or the `H` key,
showing the live per-stage breakdown that 05-01 now emits as `kmv-latency` RTVI messages.

Purpose: These are the "sees a visible countdown" and "can toggle a latency HUD" success criteria (3 & 4),
and the HUD doubles as the operator's tuning instrument. Both consume streams already flowing after 05-04.
Output: countdown + HUD wired to real session data.
</objective>

## Phase Goal

**As a** member of the public at a conference, **I want to** sign in at voice.klankermaker.ai, tap the
mic, and hold a slick low-latency conversation with the KlankerMaker concierge — with a live orb, captions,
a countdown, and honest failure handling — **so that** I experience the "whoa" of the demo and can cleanly
reconnect for another turn.
