<objective>
Extend the Phase-11 Asterisk config with a VoIP.ms registration-based trunk (D-01): an auth/registration/AOR/endpoint/identify set that registers OUTBOUND to one Toronto VoIP.ms POP so inbound DID calls arrive over the registered leg (no public inbound SIP port), land in the existing inbound-only `from-klanker-inbound` Stasis context, and negotiate ulaw only. Output: VoIP.ms sections in pjsip.conf, render_configs.py extended for VOIPMS_SIP_*, and structural-lint tests proving the posture.
</objective>
