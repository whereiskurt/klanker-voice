infrastructure; 04-03 deploys the 04-01 container onto it and proves ICE/RTP.

autoscale + scale-in protection).

custom-metric autoscaling policy.

<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| public internet -> voice task ENI (UDP media) | the wide UDP SG range is the raw media ingress surface on a public IP |
| voice task -> AWS control plane | the task role's permissions bound what a compromised task can touch |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-04-06 | Denial of service / exposure | Wide UDP SG range (was 1024-65535) on a public IP | high | mitigate | Ingress narrowed to 20000-20100/udp and the container sysctl ip_local_port_range pins aiortc into that same window so the open surface is the minimum WebRTC needs, not the whole ephemeral range (Task 1) |
| T-04-13 | Elevation of privilege | Over-broad task-role IAM (a public-facing task is the most likely compromise point) | high | mitigate | Least-privilege statements: usage-table-only DynamoDB, namespaced PutMetricData, cluster-scoped ecs task-protection, region-conditioned DescribeNetworkInterfaces — no table-wide or account-wide grants (Task 2) |
| T-04-14 | Tampering | usage-table lease items never expiring -> stale concurrency slots leak | medium | mitigate | TTL enabled on expiresAt so crashed-task heartbeats self-clean (the D-01 self-healing lease depends on this infra) (Task 2) |
| T-04-15 | Denial of service | autoscale thrash or runaway scale-out burning budget | medium | accept | max_capacity is hard-capped at 4 and the budget kill-switch (04-06) is the site-wide backstop; target_value tuned conservative; documented (Task 3) |
</threat_model>
